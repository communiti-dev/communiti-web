A hackathon is not only a platform for coding challenges but for community building also; it creates a professional work culture with positive vibes to work in. All these when merged together only one name comes up high- HackVerse .

HackVerse is a platform to build a community of a healthy youth with a bigger perspective on problem solving. Being in a tech minded country which still lack behind in terms of community building and real time problem solving. This platform enables the ability of creating a healthy community with a broader mindsets.

HackVerse will be a 24 hours Hybrid Hackathon with more than 150 members Hacking In person and every other member taking part and hacking virtually ( Both In person and Virtual teams are equally eligible for the prizes mentioned )

Now taking in about our organisers- CodeChef. It is a well known established college club which has proven it's fame and capability on every verge of testing. It has organised many big events like- Chennai's biggest Web 3.0 event: Builder's Connect managing over 700+ Web3 enthusiast with dozens of tech giants. Now it's here to exhibit it's greatest of all time in as- HackVerse on 15th & 16th of April in Chennai.
